Important checks¶

Some checks need to be performed before starting the observations.

On discos-console¶

Check that :
  • all of the 33 containers are active on ACS (MNG virtual desktop) (Fig.1);
  • the active surface is green on the AS virtual desktop (Fig.2);
. - the interface of the Meteo client is open to check the wind
velocity in real time (< 50 km/h) (Fig.6). If it is close, type $ meteoClient & on a shell.
Check the presence of the 9 panels (Fig.7):
  • operatorInput (Fig.8)
  • AntennaBoss (Fig.9)
  • GenericBackend (Fig.10)
  • Mount (Fig.12)
  • Observatory (Fig.14)
  • Receivers (Fig.15)
  • Scheduler (Fig.18)
  • MinorServo (Fig.20)
  • ACS custom logging client (Fig.5)

Upload your shedules (.scd, .lis, .bck and .cfg files) and check them:

From your computer:

$ scp  [schedulename.*] gavino@nuraghe-mng:/archive/schedules/[projectID]

On nuraghe-mng:

$ cd /archive/schedules/[projectID]

$ scheduleChecker [schedulename.scd]
