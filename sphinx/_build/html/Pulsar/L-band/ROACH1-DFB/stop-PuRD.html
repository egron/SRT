End of the session¶

> : commands to insert in the operatorInput panel

: check the execution on the monitor

Your observations are now finished, we can stop the schedule and park the antenna.

On their respective desktops¶

  1. Close the vnc session on seadas
  2. Close the vnc session on psrdfb
  3. Close the vnc session on leap0

On mng-dev¶

  1. Park the minor servo, active surface and antenna

    > goTo=180d,89d Fig.12

    > servoPark Fig.23

    > asPark Fig.3

    > antennaPark Fig.13 Fig.28

Block the axes of the antenna¶

Look at the ACU monitor, wait until Axis blocked appears in red. It can take a few minutes after the command > antennaPark has been given (Fig.29).

Only at this moment, you can press on the emergency stop button (Fig.30).
